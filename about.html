Our Story

"IF YOU CAN DREAM IT, YOU CAN DO IT."

By following the above moto we started formation of our eSports Organisation from 14th May 2021.Our Dream Is Simple i.e. to form an eSports Team of World's Best Players alongwith all the Players who Wills to Become a Successful and Renowned Gamer in their near Future.

We are the people who have an ideology of providing all the players a good roleplay experience and to ensure that we try to provide a lag free and un-interrupted roleplay exposure. our eSport's server is based upon a custom framework which is designed in a way keeping in mind the things which can provide a platform which can help all the players to establish a good character progression.
CONCLUSION: We Are An eSports Organisation Of FUTURISTIC PLAYERS.
